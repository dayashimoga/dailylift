Managing money doesn't require an MBA. These five simple rules will put you ahead of 90% of people when it comes to financial health.

## Rule 2: Build an Emergency Fund

Before investing a single rupee, build an emergency fund covering 3-6 months of expenses. Keep it in a high-yield savings account for easy access.

## Rule 3: Avoid High-Interest Debt

Credit card debt at 30-40% annual interest is financial poison. Pay off credit cards in full every month. If you have existing debt, use the avalanche method (pay highest interest first) or snowball method (pay smallest balance first).
